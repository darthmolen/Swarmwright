# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant**, version 2.1, as its code of conduct. The full
text is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: participants are expected to act professionally and respectfully, assume good intent,
and help keep the community welcoming to everyone. Harassment and abuse of any kind are not
tolerated.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainer at **darthmolen@gmail.com**. Reports will be handled
confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement

The maintainer is responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate, fair corrective
action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, up to and including a temporary or
permanent ban from project spaces. See the linked Contributor Covenant for the full enforcement
guidelines.
